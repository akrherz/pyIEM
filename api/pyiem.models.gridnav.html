pyiem.models.gridnav module#

Grid Navigation Metadata.

pydantic model pyiem.models.gridnav.CartesianGridNavigation[source]#

Bases: BaseModel

Navigation for cartesian grid with (0,0) in lower left.

The left_edge and bottom_edge are the only required fields. The rest are optional, but you need to have enough information to define the grid, ie provide dx and dy or nx and ny.

Fields:

  • bottom_edge (float)

  • crs (str | pyproj.crs.crs.CRS)

  • dy (float | None)

  • left_edge (float)

  • nx (int | None)

  • ny (int | None)

  • right_edge (float | None)
