THE NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE IN MIAMI HAS ISSUED A

* FLOOD ADVISORY FOR...
  SOUTHEASTERN BROWARD COUNTY IN SOUTHEASTERN FLORIDA...
  NORTHEASTERN MIAMI-DADE COUNTY IN SOUTHEASTERN FLORIDA...

* UNTIL 615 PM EST

* AT 313 PM EST...DOPPLER RADAR CONTINUED TO INDICATE
  HEAVY RAINS ACROSS PORTIONS OF THE AREA. AS MUCH AS 3 TO 6 INCHES
  OF RAIN WITH LOCAL AMOUNTS IN EXCESS OF 8 INCHES HAVE BEEN ESTIMATED
  OR OBSERVED ACROSS THE AREA WITH HEAVY RAINS CONTINUING AT THIS TIME.
  THE AREA GETTING HIT THE HARDEST AT THIS TIME IS SOUTHEAST BROWARD
  COUNTY WHERE ADDITIONAL RAINFALL AMOUNTS OVER THE COMING HOUR COULD
  STILL EXCEED 3 INCHES. ELSEWHERE LIGHTER RAINFALL AMOUNTS ARE
  FALLING AT THIS TIME BUT DUE TO STANDING WATER ON ROADWAYS AND
  THE FACT THAT IT IS STILL RAINING THE ADVISORY CONTINUES IN
  EFFECT ACROSS THE AREA.

  IN THE SHORT TERM THERE IS THE CONCERN THAT THE ADVISORY
  FOR PORTIONS OF SOUTHEAST BROWARD MIGHT NEED TO BE UPGRADED
  TO A WARNING SHOULD RAINS NOT CEASE. SO STAY TUNED TO THE LATEST
  INFORMATION.

* SOME LOCATIONS THAT WILL CONTINUE TO EXPERIENCE STREET FLOODING INCLUDE...
  MIAMI...LIBERTY CITY...MIAMI BEACH...OPA LOCKA...
  NORTH MIAMI BEACH...AND HALLANDALE INCLUDING COMMUNITIES
  IN BETWEEN AND/OR ADJACENT TO THEM.

BE DISCOUNTED OVER AREAS WHERE RAIN CONTINUES TO STALL COMPOUNDING TO

AREAS AND LOW LYING SPOTS.
