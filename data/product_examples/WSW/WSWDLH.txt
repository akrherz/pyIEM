...WINTER WEATHER ADVISORY IN EFFECT FROM 1 AM TO 1 PM CDT
MONDAY...

* WHAT...Mixed precipitation expected. Total ice accumulations of
  around one tenth of an inch and snow accumulations of up to one
  inch. Winds gusting as high as 45 mph.

* WHERE...Northern Cook and Lake and Southern Cook Counties.
  This includes the Tribal Lands of the Grand Portage
  Reservation.

* WHEN...From 1 AM to 1 PM CDT Monday.

* IMPACTS...Plan on slippery road conditions on the Gunflint
  Trail. Gusty winds could bring down tree branches.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS...Main concern is across the higher terrain
  of the North Shore. Not expecting ice accumulations on Highway
  61 or locales adjacent to Lake Superior. The Gunflint Trail
  should be impacted though. Road temperatures may be warm enough
  to limit ice accumulations.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Slow down and use caution while traveling.

Road conditions can also be found at 511mn.org for Minnesota or
511wi.gov for Wisconsin.
