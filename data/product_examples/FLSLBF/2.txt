...FLOOD ADVISORY FOR ICE JAM FLOODING REMAINS IN EFFECT FOR CENTRAL
LINCOLN COUNTY UNTIL 100 PM CST THURSDAY...

AT 10 AM CST...MINOR FLOODING OF AREAS ALONG THE SOUTH PLATTE RIVER 
JUST EAST OF NORTH PLATTE CONTINUES. AN ICE JAM NEAR THE CONFLUENCE 
OF THE NORTH AND SOUTH PLATTE RIVERS HAS CAUSED WATER TO BACK UP AND 
RISE OUT OF THE RIVER BANK EAST OF THE CITY OF NORTH PLATTE IN THE 
VICINITY OF NEWBERRY ACCESS ROAD AND THE IRON EAGLE GOLF COURSE. THE 
MINOR FLOODING WILL BE LIMITED TO LOW LYING AREAS ALONG THE RIVER.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

REPORT FLOODING TO THE NEAREST LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCY SO THAT THEY
MAY TAKE APPROPRIATE ACTION TO CLOSE FLOODED ROADWAYS. THEY WILL
RELAY YOUR REPORT TO THE NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE IN NORTH PLATTE.
