...FLOOD ADVISORY IN EFFECT UNTIL 1215 PM CHST...

* WHAT...Urban and small stream flooding caused by excessive
  rainfall is expected.

* WHEN...Until 1215 PM CHST.

* IMPACTS...Minor flooding in low-lying and poor drainage areas.
  Water over roadways. Overflowing poor drainage areas.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS...
  - At 1246 AM UTC, Satellite imagery shows heavy rain
    approaching Tinian Island from the south. This will cause
    urban and small stream flooding. Overflowing poor drainage
    areas will cause minor flooding in Tinian Island.
  - Rainfall amounts of 2 to 3 inches are expected over the area.
    This rain will result in minor flooding.
  - Some locations that will experience flooding include...
    San Jose (Tinian Municipality), Marpo Heights, Carolinas
    Heights, Eastern Tinian (Marpo Valley), Carolinas, Western
    Tinian and Northern Tinian.
