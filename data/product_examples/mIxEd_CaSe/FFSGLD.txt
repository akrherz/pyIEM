...THE FLASH FLOOD WARNING REMAINS IN EFFECT UNTIL 700 PM MDT FOR
SOUTH CENTRAL CHEYENNE COUNTY IN EAST CENTRAL COLORADO...

At 541 PM MDT, trained weather spotters reported thunderstorms
producing heavy rain in central Cheyenne County in Colorado. Water
was flowing across rural cross roads with several creeks now free
flowing.

Some locations that will experience flooding include...
Kit Carson and Firstview.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Turn around, don't drown when encountering flooded roads. Most flood
deaths occur in vehicles.

A Flash Flood Warning means that flooding is imminent or occurring.
If you are in the warned area move to higher ground immediately.
Residents living along streams and creeks should take immediate
precautions to protect life and property.
